# Exploring Crash data for New York City

Motor_Vehicle_Collisions_Dataset
Data is crash data from 2012 to 2025 in New York City where someone is injured or killed, or where there is at least $1000 worth of damage.
Using this data I want to answer questions like who, what, when, where , and why do  poeple have acidents involving motor vehicles

## FAQ

#### When does the data take place?

11/2012 to 11/2025

#### What does the data cover?

The data covers Crash date/Time. Where the crash took place. Number of people involded as well as if they where walking, driving , or on a bicycle. If the persons ivolved where injured or killed. Contributing factors to the crash and finally what type of vehicle was involved.
